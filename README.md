🎯 What It Does

- **Order Management** – Create, update, and track cake orders (status, due date, customer name).
- **Product/Inventory Tracking** – Manage available cakes, ingredients, and stock levels.
- **Customer Records** – Store customer contacts and order history.
- **Simple Dashboard** – View daily/weekly summaries.
- **Exportable Reports** – CSV sales reports for tax or record‑keeping.
- **Bootstrap 5 UI** – Clean, responsive, mobile‑friendly (works on a baker’s phone in the kitchen).
